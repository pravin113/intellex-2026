// ABOUT THE EVENT

Redefining the Symposium Experience

INTELLEX 2026 is not just another college symposium — it's a convergence of the brightest minds, the boldest ideas, and the most cutting-edge technology.

Spanning two action-packed days, INTELLEX brings together students from across the nation to compete, collaborate, and create. From intense coding battles to creative showcases, from thought-provoking paper presentations to hands-on workshops — there's something for every innovator.

Whether you're a tech wizard, a creative genius, or a strategic thinker, INTELLEX 2026 is your arena. Step up, stand out, and leave your mark.
